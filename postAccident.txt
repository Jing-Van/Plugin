2. 
Medical and rehabilitation expenses, up to a maximum of $150,000.
Wage loss benefits, up to a maximum of $300 / week and only to claimants who were totally disabled from employment within 20 days of the accident. 
Wage loss benefits are payable for a maximum of 104 weeks.
